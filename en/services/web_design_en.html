The usability of the website

The usability for visitors of a website is our most important criterion. Complicated websites are not easy to use. With a simple web design with a basic and flawless design, the usability of the site can be increased. The user-friendly interface (UI) is very important to provide the user a suitable experience (UX).

Functionality of the website

Is the web design in conformity with the goals of the company or the persons? Is the website functional? We ask ourselves such questions and realize our design in this framework.

Simplicity of the website

Nowadays simplicity is one of the most important concepts in web design. Designs that are not simple and have a complicated structure do not in any way catch the attention of users. Simple and attractive designs, on the other hand, do catch the attention of visitors.

Mobile compatible responsive website

Mobile compatibility is one of the necessary elements of a website. Nowadays many users visit websites from their mobile devices. For the best user experience, we believe it is important that the website has mobile compatibility. 75% Of the website visits takes place via mobile devices.

Design stages of the website

The web design services take place in different stages. We can outline the web design stages of MediaFPV as follows.

  • First of all, the needs, goals and objectives of the customer are clearly defined. Within the framework of the relevant needs, strategic planning is realized with regard to what kind of web design the customer wants.
  • Subsequently, a preliminary design is made that corresponds to the identity and colors of the company or person as part of this strategic planning.
  • The created preliminary design is offered to the customer and if the customer requests to revise it, the design is revised and the new design is presented to the customer.
  • After confirmation by the customer, integration to the dashboard is provided.
  • The content submitted by customers and the supporting content requested from the agency is added to the panel and the customer is sent a demo link.
  • The customer can view the website via the link and, if necessary, ask for adjustments to be made. After the necessary revision and confirmation, the website is published
