Photos and videos that can be taken with smart phones that everyone has nowadays are very different from those of a photo artist or a composition expert.

For impressive results in the field of photo or video, creative ideas of the mind, the necessary equipment in the technical field and the impeccable knowledge about the use of those materials are necessary.

Products within the framework of these elements nowadays facilitate the growth of brand value. In contrast, activities that emphasize the unique mission and vision are the only opportunity to differentiate from other brands, with hundreds of new brands coming up every day. Animated films

Nowadays, 3 different types of animation are widely used, namely 2D animation, 3D animation and whiteboard animation. They are widely used in the production of many contents such as animated films, cartoons, marketing videos, commercials, educational materials and games.

Since video content became popular, more businesses consider using animated videos for advertising. These types of videos increase their conversion rate by roughly 20%. Some companies work with specialized media companies for animation, while others try to create them online with the help of video tools.

Animation videos have a great impact on viewers in visual, auditory and kinesthetic ways. Besides, they also look very cute. That's why they are preferred.
