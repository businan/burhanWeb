Use of Drones in Advertisements and The Agricultural Sector

Since drone technology forms a part of all aspects of our lives, it has also brought a brand new advertising concept: Drone Advertising!

Use of Drones in Advertisements

The perception of bringing a new perspective to advertising and creating unforgettable and memorable advertisements, started to spread at an unceasing speed. Drone Advertising, which has emerged as a successful entrepreneurship movement, will penetrate into every sector within the short term.

Not only has it become an advertising type that attracts the attention of every brand, it is also remarkable, catchy and a successful type of advertisement in any project.

Use of drones in the agricultural sector

The use of drones in agricultural activities is increasing day by day.

Agricultural drones are used in many areas including:

  • Soil and Area Analysis
  • Control of Plant Pollination
  • Weed and Disease Control
  • Crop Mapping and Surveying
  • Seed Planting
  • Spot Spraying for crops
  • Irrigation Monitoring and Management
  • Real Time Livestock Monitoring
